About Us

Bridged is a non-profit tutoring platform providing a digital plane where parents/guardians of elementary and secondary school students can reach out to TUM students to receive tutoring. The academic support provided is not strictly limited to German school-related course subjects but also floods into the passions and interests of the tutors, which may be shared by the younger learners. As individuals engulfed in academia, we tutors wish to share that excitement with the next generation of young scholars.

Our Mission

Bridged believes in supporting students cross the academic gap rooted in systemic educational inequalities. Regardless of culture, language, and academic profile, we strive to provide all students with proper opportunities to achieve educational success.

Why Bridged?

With the non-German population growing in Heilbronn, it remains essential that students of all backgrounds have access to affordable education tailored to their needs, independent of linguistic, religious, and cultural barriers. At Bridged, we overcome these obstacles by ensuring that every student is matched with a tutor stemming from a similar socio-geographical background, and guarantee the best learning environment for the student.
