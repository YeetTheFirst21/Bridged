Mission Statement

Bridged believes in supporting students cross the academic gap rooted in systemic educational inequalities. Regardless of culture, language, and academic profile, we strive to provide all students with proper opportunities to achieve educational success.

About Us

Bridged is a TUM student-led non-profit initiative striving to create a digital platform where secondary students can reach out to and receive tutoring from TUM students. As individuals engulfed in academia, we tutors wish to share that excitement with the next generation of young scholars.

How It Works

TUM students will provide you or your children with academic support to foster their educational journey. The tutoring will be offered online, in person, or hybrid. The course and language of instruction will be decided by you.
